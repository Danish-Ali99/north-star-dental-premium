Discreet Alignment With Invisalign

Invisalign uses a series of custom-printed clear plastic aligners, each worn for 1–2 weeks, to move your teeth into the planned position. There are no brackets, no wires, and almost no one can tell you’re wearing them.

Treatment begins with a digital intra-oral scan — no goopy impressions — and a 3D preview of your finished smile before you commit. Most cases finish in 6 to 18 months, with simpler cases as quick as 4 months.

Aligners come out for eating, drinking, and brushing, so no foods are off-limits and your hygiene routine doesn’t change. Visits are quick and roughly every 6–8 weeks.
