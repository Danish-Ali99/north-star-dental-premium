About Dr. Vartika

Orthodontist & Dentofacial Orthopaedist · BDS, MDS

Dr. Vartika Kumari is the founder and lead orthodontist at Dr V Dental & Aesthetics. A Gold Medalist in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics, she brings together clinical excellence, attention to detail, and a genuine passion for patient care.

Originally from Varanasi and now based in Bangalore, Dr. Vartika completed her BDS from Manipal College of Dental Sciences and went on to pursue her MDS in Orthodontics from A.B. Shetty Memorial Institute of Dental Sciences, where she graduated with top honours. Over the years, she has treated hundreds of patients with a wide range of orthodontic concerns, from simple alignment corrections to complex bite problems, impacted teeth, clear aligner treatments, and multidisciplinary rehabilitation cases.

As a Certified Invisalign Provider, she believes that orthodontic treatment should not only create beautiful smiles but also improve oral health, function, and long-term confidence. Her treatment philosophy focuses on individualized care, honest communication, and delivering results that are both aesthetically pleasing and clinically sound.

Beyond dentistry, Dr. Vartika is passionate about building a practice where patients feel comfortable, informed, and cared for at every stage of their treatment journey. Her warm and approachable nature, combined with her commitment to continuous learning, has helped her earn the trust of families across Bangalore.

Whether treating a child needing early intervention, a teenager starting braces, or an adult seeking a smile transformation, Dr. Vartika is dedicated to helping every patient achieve their best smile with confidence.
