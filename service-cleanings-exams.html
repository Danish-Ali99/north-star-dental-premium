Why Regular Cleanings & Exams Matter

Even the best home brushing and flossing leaves behind tartar that only a trained hygienist can remove. Our cleanings polish away surface stains, clear buildup from the gumline, and leave your mouth genuinely clean.

During your exam, our dentists check every surface of each tooth, screen for early signs of gum disease, oral cancer, and bite problems — and take targeted X-rays only when clinically necessary.

Catching a cavity when it is small means a simple filling. Finding it later can mean a crown or root canal. Regular visits simply cost less — in time, money, and discomfort.
