About Dr. Shalini

Pediatric Dentist · RGUHS

Dr. Shalini Shinde is a dedicated Pediatric Dentist who has done her graduation and post graduation under RGUHS. She comes with over 12 yrs of specialised clinical experience. Her passion is providing tailored gentle care for children with diverse medical, physical and developmental needs. Dr. Shalini is trained in providing comprehensive dental rehabilitation under General Anaesthesia. Every visit ensures a lifetime of healthy smiles.
