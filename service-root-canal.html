Relieving Tooth Pain With Root Canal Treatment in Bengaluru

When the pulp inside a tooth becomes inflamed or infected — from deep decay, repeated dental work, or a crack — a root canal removes the source of the pain without removing the tooth itself.

Using rotary endodontics and magnification, our team cleans, shapes, and seals the canals with precision. Most patients tell us the procedure is no different from a routine filling — and they walk out comfortable.

A well-restored root canal tooth, capped with a crown, can serve you for decades. It’s almost always the kinder, longer-lasting choice over extraction.

Frequently Asked Questions About Root Canal Treatment in Bengaluru

Modern root canal treatment is performed under strong local anaesthesia and is typically no more uncomfortable than a regular filling. The pain you feel beforehand is the infection — the procedure relieves it.
Most root canals are completed in a single 60–90 minute appointment. Complex molars or curved canals may need a second short visit a week later.
With a well-fitted crown placed on top, a root canal-treated tooth can last 15+ years — often a lifetime. Routine cleanings and good home care matter most.
For back teeth, almost always. Root canal-treated teeth become more brittle and a crown protects them from cracking. Front teeth sometimes need only a strong bonded filling.
