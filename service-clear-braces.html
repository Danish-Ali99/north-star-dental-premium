Reliable Alignment, Quietly Done

Clear braces use ceramic brackets that match the colour of your teeth and a thin tooth-coloured wire, making the appliance much less visible than traditional metal. The mechanics are exactly the same — just the appearance is softer.

They handle a wider range of corrections than aligners, including some complex rotations and bite issues, and don’t depend on patient compliance like removable trays do.

Treatment usually takes 12 to 24 months, with adjustments roughly every 4–6 weeks. Modern self-ligating brackets mean fewer visits and gentler movements than older systems.
