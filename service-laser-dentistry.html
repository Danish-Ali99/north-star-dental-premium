Modern Treatment with Dental Lasers

Lasers are now the standard of care for many soft-tissue and gum procedures — from contouring an uneven gumline to treating early gum disease, removing benign lesions, or aiding implant healing.

Compared to traditional methods, laser treatment is often quieter, requires less or no anaesthetic, and produces almost no bleeding. Patients are usually surprised by how little discomfort there is afterwards.

We use both diode and Er:YAG lasers depending on the tissue, with calibrated settings for each procedure. The result: precise outcomes, faster healing, and fewer follow-up visits.
