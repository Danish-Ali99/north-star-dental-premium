Protecting Your Teeth with a Custom-Fitted Guard

Many people grind or clench their teeth during sleep without realising it. Over time, bruxism wears down enamel, cracks teeth, and strains the jaw joint. A night guard sits between your teeth and absorbs the force before it causes damage.

Sports mouth guards protect teeth, gums, lips, and jaw from impact during contact sports. A custom-fitted guard offers significantly better protection than a standard boil-and-bite option from a pharmacy — and is far more comfortable to wear.

Both types are made from impressions of your teeth, ensuring a precise fit that stays in place and does not interfere with breathing. A guard that does not fit properly simply does not get worn.
