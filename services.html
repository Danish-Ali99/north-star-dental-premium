Our Services

Care for every chapter of your smile.

Twenty-five treatments covering everything from a simple cleaning to a full mouth rehabilitation. Tap any service to see what it involves.

Home / Services
Professional in-clinic and take-home bleaching that lifts years of coffee, tea, and tobacco stains in a single visit. Safe enamel-friendly gels supervised by a dentist — never a salon.
A coordinated combination of treatments — whitening, veneers, alignment, gum reshaping — designed around your face, not just your teeth. We plan every case twice before touching a tooth.
Ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth — instantly correcting shape, colour, and minor alignment. Hand-finished to disappear into your smile.
Customised aesthetic design tailored to your face shape, lip line, age, and personality. We design smiles that look like they belong to you — never generic.
See your final smile before treatment begins — using 3D digital previews and intraoral scanning. You approve the design first; we build to it.
Permanent titanium replacements for missing teeth — surgically placed and topped with a porcelain crown. Restores full chewing function and a natural-looking smile.
Modern, pain-free endodontics that saves an infected tooth — performed under microscope, often in a single sitting. Nothing like the old reputation.
Tooth-coloured composite fillings that repair cavities seamlessly. Layered and polished by hand so even you can't see where the filling ends and the tooth begins.
Gentle removal of damaged or impacted teeth, including wisdom teeth. Sedation available for anxious patients or complex cases — most patients walk out the same day.
Restore damaged or missing teeth with porcelain crowns and bridges that look and feel natural. Same-day ceramics available — no temporary, no second visit.
Custom-fitted full or partial dentures that restore chewing, speech, and confidence. Implant-retained options available for the most secure fit.
Complete restoration combining implants, crowns, veneers, and bite correction — for total oral health, function, and aesthetics. Phased over weeks, planned over months.
Thorough professional cleaning that removes plaque and tartar from places your brush can't reach. The unglamorous foundation of every healthy mouth — done thoroughly, never rushed.
A 30-minute exam, digital X-rays, and a written treatment plan — quiet, unhurried, with no obligation to proceed. We'll explain what's necessary, what's optional, and what can wait.
Personalised diagnosis and care for halitosis — addressing the root cause, not the symptom. Most cases trace back to a treatable oral or sinus issue.
Lasting relief from sensitivity caused by exposed roots, enamel wear, or gum recession. We diagnose the underlying cause first, then treat the trigger — not just the symptom.
Targeted care for recurring ulcers, canker sores, and oral lesions — with healing protocols, dietary guidance, and laser therapy where appropriate.
Straighten your smile with traditional braces or virtually invisible Invisalign aligners. Every case planned in 3D from day one — you see the final result before the first tray is fitted.
Comprehensive treatment for bleeding gums, gum disease, and periodontal infections. Deep cleaning, laser therapy, and surgical options when needed — most cases reverse with the right care.
Precise, virtually painless treatment using advanced dental lasers for gums, cavities, and soft tissue. Less bleeding, faster healing, often no anaesthesia required.
Anxiety-free treatment with oral sedation, nitrous oxide, or IV options — for nervous patients, complex work, or anyone who'd simply rather sleep through it.
Diagnosis and care for jaw joint pain, clicking, locking, and chronic facial discomfort. Custom night guards, bite correction, and physiotherapy referrals where appropriate.
Custom oral appliances that quiet snoring and treat mild-to-moderate sleep apnea — a comfortable alternative to CPAP for the right candidates.
Friendly, gentle care for children from age three onwards — first visits, cleanings, fluoride, sealants, and habit counselling. Stickers and smiles guaranteed; tears, rare.
Same-day appointments for severe pain, broken teeth, knocked-out teeth, or trauma. Pain shouldn't wait — call us, or message on WhatsApp, and we'll find space in the schedule.
